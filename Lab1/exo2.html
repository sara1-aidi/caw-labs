Main Content

Since October 7, 2023, Gaza has been under brutal aggression resulting in the deaths of more than 40,000 martyrs, including thousands of children and women. Infrastructure, hospitals, schools, and homes have been destroyed.

The blockade has caused severe shortages of food, clean water, and medicine. According to Sehatty and Al Jazeera, the humanitarian situation continues to deteriorate.

The famine and the lack of international response have led to catastrophic consequences for the population, especially children who suffer from malnutrition and trauma.

During the war in Gaza, thousands of homes were destroyed and many families were displaced as a result of continuous bombing.
