Brief History

The State of Palestine has a long and complex history dating back thousands of years. It has been a center of culture, trade, and religion throughout history.

In the 20th century, Palestine became the heart of the Arab–Israeli conflict. The region witnessed wars, displacement, and a continuous struggle for independence.

Today, Palestinians continue to fight for their freedom, dignity, and recognition as an independent state, with Al-Quds as their capital.
